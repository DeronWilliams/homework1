Maps


Overwatch has special designed maps that can help other characters benfit from their use. For example, some maps are huge,so some players may pick snipers like windowmaker or hanzo. These characters are huge on these maps, but can be easieler countered by close up combat characters like Winston. There are also some maps like: Nepal that benfits close up fighting while in the temple. This map can help characters like Doomfist. There are a total of 16 maps in OW and they are all unique in their own way.
